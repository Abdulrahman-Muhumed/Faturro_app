Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Profile & Company Details: Company name, address, logo.
  • Customers & Expenses: Customer names, contact info, expense descriptions, amounts, dates, payment methods.
  • Invoices: Invoice numbers, dates, line-item details, taxes, discounts, status (visible only to the owner).

1.2 Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: App opens, feature usage (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Device & Log Data: IP address, device type, OS version, crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide & Improve Services: Authenticate you, store and manage your data.
  • Communications: Send receipts, notifications, important updates.
  • Security & Compliance: Detect fraud, troubleshoot, enforce terms, comply with law.
  • Analytics & Research: Aggregate data to analyze trends and usage.

3. Sharing of Information

We share data only with:

  • Service Providers (e.g., Firebase, hosting, analytics) to operate the App.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We use SSL/TLS in transit, Firebase Realtime Database rules, and other safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure—please use strong passwords and keep your device updated.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and transactional data as long as needed to provide services. You may request deletion, subject to legal or compliance requirements.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: View or update your profile, customers, and invoices in the App.
  • Deletion: Contact us to delete your account and personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their personal data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We’ll notify you via the App or email before material changes take effect.
